# Your dashboard

The dashboard is the 30-second view. Open it, glance at it, close it. Anything that needs more attention has a deeper page behind it.

## Top row — the four stat tiles

Across the top you'll see four numbers, each with a comparison vs. the prior month:

- **Net worth** — assets minus liabilities, right now. The big one.
- **Income (this month)** — money coming in: salary, transfers in, refunds.
- **Expenses (this month)** — money going out, excluding internal transfers between your own accounts.
- **Cash flow (this month)** — income minus expenses. Green if positive, red if negative.

The percentage delta next to each number compares to the same point in the prior month, so early in the month the deltas swing wildly — that's expected.

## Balance overview chart

A daily line chart of your net worth over the last 30 days. Each point is a snapshot taken at end-of-day by a [scheduled job](/docs/developers/architecture), so the line keeps moving even when you don't open the app.

Hover any point to see the exact value and date. The dashboard chart is the short version of the full picture — for the year-or-more view, jump to [Net worth](/docs/developers/architecture#net-worth-snapshots).

## Category breakdown

A bar chart of where your expenses went this month, grouped by category. The categories themselves come from Plaid by default but you can override them — see [Categorization & rules](/docs/categorization).

Categories are sorted largest first. The "Other" bucket sweeps up anything below the top 8 categories so the chart stays readable.

## Recent transactions

The last ten transactions across **all** linked accounts. Click any row to open the full transaction record where you can:

- Recategorize it (and optionally turn that into a rule).
- Add a note.
- See the underlying Plaid data.

For the full searchable list, go to **Transactions** in the sidebar.

## Personal vs. household scope

If you've joined a household, you'll see a **scope switcher** at the top right that toggles between:

- **Personal** — all your accounts, shared and private.
- **Household** — only accounts you and your partner have both opted to share.

The dashboard recomputes every tile and chart based on the selected scope. Your private accounts never appear in the household view, even when you're the one looking at it.

See [Households](/docs/households) for the full story.

## What's not on the dashboard

- **Budgets** — the dashboard shows spending in aggregate, not against limits. For budget progress, open the **Budget** page.
- **Individual accounts** — the dashboard shows totals; for per-account balances and recent activity, open **Accounts**.
- **Goals** — savings progress lives on the **Goals** page.

The dashboard is intentionally a summary. If you find yourself wanting a number that isn't there, it almost always exists on its dedicated page.
