# Getting started

This is the five-minute path from "I just got my invite" to "I'm looking at my own dashboard."

## 1. Accept your invite

Open the invite email and click the **Create account** link. It opens `/sign-up` with a token baked in — that token is single-use, so finish setup in the same tab you opened it in.

<Callout type="note">
  The email in the form is locked to the address you joined the waitlist with. If you want a different email, reply to the invite — we'll reissue.
</Callout>

Set a password (8+ characters) and submit. Supabase will send a confirmation link to that email; click it to verify, and you'll land on the sign-in page.

## 2. Sign in

Use the email and password you just set. You'll land on the empty **Dashboard** at `/dashboard`. It's blank because you haven't linked any accounts yet.

## 3. Link your first bank

From the dashboard, hit **Accounts** in the sidebar and then **Link an institution**. Plaid handles the actual bank handshake — pick your bank, log in with your bank credentials, and Plaid hands Fincenzo back a list of accounts.

Pick which of those accounts you want to track. You can always toggle individual accounts on or off later.

<Callout type="tip">
  Your bank credentials never touch Fincenzo's servers. Plaid sees them; we don't. What we receive is an access token, encrypted at rest before it's written to the database.
</Callout>

## 4. Wait for the sync

The first sync pulls the last ~90 days of transactions plus your current balances. It happens in the background — usually under a minute. The dashboard will start filling in as soon as the first batch lands.

## 5. (Optional) Set up budgets and goals

Once transactions are in, you can:

- Open **Budget** and create monthly category budgets.
- Open **Goals** and set savings targets (e.g. "Emergency fund: $10,000").
- Open **Net worth** to see the running chart — daily snapshots start immediately.
