# Fincenzo docs

Fincenzo is a budgeting and net-worth tracker for individuals and the couples they live with. These docs are the practical guide to getting set up, finding your way around, and inviting a partner without losing your privacy.

If you're brand new, start at [Getting started](/docs/getting-started). If you're trying to do one specific thing, the sidebar groups everything by topic.

## What you'll find here

- **Getting started** — sign up, link your first account, see your first dashboard.
- **Personal finance** — how budgets, goals, categorization, and net worth tracking work day to day.
- **Households** — invite a partner, share what you want, keep the rest private.
- **Privacy & data** — what's encrypted, what Plaid sees, how to delete your account.
- **Developers** — architecture, local setup, and how things actually work under the hood.

## Built in the open

Fincenzo is being built by one person, in public. The full source lives at [github.com/xvrgill/fincenzo](https://github.com/xvrgill/fincenzo), and the [roadmap](/docs/developers/roadmap) lives right here in the docs. If something is unclear or wrong, [open an issue](https://github.com/xvrgill/fincenzo/issues).
