# Privacy & data

This page explains what we store, where, and how — with no hand-waving. If anything here is unclear, [open an issue](https://github.com/xvrgill/fincenzo/issues).

## What Plaid sees vs. what Fincenzo sees

When you link a bank, you log in **on a Plaid-hosted screen**. Your bank credentials never reach Fincenzo's servers.

After authentication, Plaid hands Fincenzo:

- A long-lived **access token** for the institution (encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM before we store it).
- A list of **accounts** with masked numbers, names, and types.
- **Transactions** and **balances**, refreshed on a Plaid webhook.

We never see your bank password and we never receive your full account or card numbers.

## What's stored in our database

| Data | Stored | Encrypted at rest |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Email + auth | Supabase Auth | Yes (Supabase-managed) |
| Plaid access tokens | `plaid_items.access_token` | Yes (AES-256-GCM) |
| Account metadata | `accounts` | No (not sensitive) |
| Transactions | `transactions` | No (not sensitive) |
| Budgets / goals | `budgets`, `goals` | No |
| Household membership | `household_members` | No |

The encryption key is held in an environment variable (`ENCRYPTION_KEY`), separate from the database.

## Sharing is opt-in, per-account

By default, **nothing** is shared. When you create or join a household:

- Every account starts as **private**.
- You flip a per-account toggle to share it with the household.
- Your partner only ever sees accounts you've explicitly shared.
- Un-sharing is instant and reversible.

There is no "auto-share" mode. There is no setting that shares everything. The only way data crosses between users is one account at a time, with your hand on the switch.

## Deleting your account

Open **Settings → Account → Delete account**. This:

1. Unlinks every Plaid item (no more webhooks).
2. Hard-deletes your transactions, accounts, budgets, goals, and snapshots.
3. Removes you from any household.
4. Deletes your Supabase auth user.

There is no soft-delete — your data is gone, not flagged for deletion. Backups roll off on the standard Supabase retention schedule.

<Callout type="warn">
  If you're the **owner** of a household, deleting your account will leave your partner without a household. Transfer ownership first, or your partner will be moved to the "no household" state.
</Callout>

## What we don't do

- We don't sell or share your data.
- We don't have an ad business, an analytics business, or a "partners" page.
- We don't use your transactions to train ML models.
- We don't run third-party trackers on the marketing page or the docs.
